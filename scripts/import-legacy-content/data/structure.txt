Teams. Every team has positions, and every position is either an executive, a commissioner or a coordinator. Since we only have 1 version of each team, it must include every position that's ever been on it, regardless of whether that position still exists or not.
# Example: VP internal was removed this year, but it should still be added because it was on the team in the past, and some people on the team had that position.

Committees: Committees assign people to positions in teams that were active that year. A person can only be on one team per year, but they can be on multiple committees.
A committee is associated with a team, and it has a name and a list of positions that it assigns to. For example, the "IEEE 2023-2024 Committee" assigns people to the positions on the IEEE team for the 2023-2024 year.
